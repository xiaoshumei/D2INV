ANALYZING FOOTBALL MATCH OUTCOMES

Top European Leagues in Focus

This infographic examines football match outcomes across key European leagues, spotlighting trends in wins, goals, high-scoring matches, goalless ties, and home advantage.

What is the overall distribution of home and away wins?

Across all leagues, home teams won 44% of the matches, while away teams won 28% of the matches. The remaining 28% of the matches ended in a draw.

Which league had the highest average goals per match?

The English Premier League had the highest average goals per match at 2.8, followed by the Bundesliga with 2.7, Serie A with 2.4, and La Liga with 2.3 goals per match on average.

What are the notable high-scoring matches?

Some notable high-scoring matches include Real Madrid's 9-1 victory over Granada in the 2014-2015 La Liga season and Bayer Leverkusen's 6-2 win over Stuttgart in the 2013-2014 Bundesliga season.

How do teams from different leagues perform in matches with no goals?

In the English Premier League, 12% of matches ended with no goals, while in Serie A, this figure was 15%. The Bundesliga had the lowest percentage of goalless matches at 9%, whereas La Liga was at 11%.

Which teams had the most significant home advantage?

Teams like Barcelona, Bayern Munich, and Juventus showcased a prominent home advantage with a win percentage of over 70% in their home games.
